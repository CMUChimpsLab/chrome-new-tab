Getting started on Chrome New Tab

Chrome New Tab is a tool that helps you with privacy management in a few simple steps:
- You answer a question about Facebook privacy settings and tell us your preferred option
- We analyze the decisions made by the crowd and find the most popular option
- We present the result to you, and you can choose to change your Facebook privacy setting in just one click!

Please note that we will collect your cookies on Facebook.com to display your current privacy settings. By using our extension, we assume that you give us permission to use your Facebook cookies.

Simply open the New Tab and answer a question to get started!
